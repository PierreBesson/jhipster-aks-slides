Why use managed kubernetes ?

    • Easy to get started and deploy immediately
    • Guaranteed to have HA on all components
    • Delegate the hard stuff: networking, storage, security
    • Less time spent on infrastructure = more time spent on business features
    • Plug into other cloud services (managed databases)

Rise of the *KS

Google GKS, Microsoft AKS, Amazon EKS
    • Spin up a cluster in a few minutes
    • Monitor your cluster and your apps
    • Control costs
    • Manage credentials and Access Control
    • Scale and upgrade your cluster
